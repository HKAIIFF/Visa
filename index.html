1. Executive Summary

Hong Kong AI International Film Festival Limited (Registration No: 77536113) is an innovative enterprise registered in Hong Kong on December 31, 2024, dedicated to building the world's first AI-native film and television content ecosystem.

The company operates six core platforms forming a complete industrial value chain: HKAIIFF Film Festival, AIF.Academy Education Platform, AIF.Market Trading Platform, AIF.Center Submission Center, 404.world Virtual Platform, and StarOS Digital Management Platform.

This project pioneers the integration of AI technology across the entire film industry value chain - from content creation, professional training, market transactions to global distribution. Hong Kong's unique position makes it irreplaceable for this venture's success.

4. Project Overview & Core Value

4.1 Industry Background

Artificial intelligence is bringing revolutionary changes to the film and television industry. From content creation to post-production, from distribution to viewing experience, AI technology is reshaping every link of the industry chain.

AI Film Industry Challenges:

  • Lack of unified quality standards and evaluation systems
  • Severe shortage of professional talent
  • Poor investment and financing channels
  • Imperfect copyright protection mechanisms
  • Absence of global distribution networks
